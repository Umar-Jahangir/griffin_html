The Griffin Club VIT Pune

Welcome to the Griffin Club - Elevate your dreams with us!

Griffin is a club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune which focuses on AERO DESIGNS. Our Extremely skilled aeronautics team can build any aircraft from scratch and compete amongst Top National Teams in the SAE Aero Design Challenges.


Why we do Aeronautical Engineering?
We believe that every young enthusiast has the capability of making something new and innovative. Team Griffin is the home where we get to make and explore the world never seen before and what it take for your model to reach the skies.
